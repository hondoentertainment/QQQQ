Methodology & disclosures

Data sources
Holdings and index weights come from the official Invesco QQQ holdings file, Financial Modeling Prep when configured, or the latest SEC N-PORT filing as a third fallback. Quotes come from Financial Modeling Prep or Yahoo Finance.
Update cadence
A scheduled job refreshes the data every 30 minutes during US market hours, plus a post-close snapshot. This page re-reads the committed data every 60 seconds.
Price latency
Quotes are delayed by up to ~15 minutes (free market-data tiers). This is not a real-time trading feed.
Metric definitions
Weight — a holding’s share of index weight. Day % — price change versus the prior close. MoM Δ — change in index weight versus the prior month. Top-10 concentration — combined weight of the 10 largest holdings. Herfindahl index — the sum of squared percentage weights (higher means more concentrated). Fund performance — the QQQ closing price recorded once per refresh day.
Data freshness
A “data may be stale” badge appears when the latest snapshot is more than 72 hours old.
